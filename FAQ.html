Frequently Asked Questions


What to do after registration?

Login your account with your user id and password. On your dashboard you will get information for payment of 500 rupees. This payment goes to your sponsor. Contact your sponsor, give500 rupees to your sponsor. After getting payment your sponsor will upgrade your ID to first level.

Is it compulsory to refer minimum 2 person?

Yes. All upgrade systems depend on your earning. You can upgrade to next level if your earning is greater than or equal to the upgrade amount. If you do not refer two people, your earning will not be sufficient to upgrade for to the second level.

There are some wrong in my profile. How do I correct them?

Send details to update, along with your user ID, from your registered mobile number or Email Id on (+91) 9360623963 (WhatsApp) or s2feducationaltrust@gmail.com

Which organisation is behind this concept, how can I get legal documents?

This concept is started in the year 2019, by “ S2F“ an NGO from Chennai. You can get all legal documents from website (About us --- Legal documents)

How will I get payment ?

All payments are member to member. You will get payment, when your downline pay you for their upgrade. After getting payment, you have to upgrade them by clicking on “I accept” button on payment slip, on your dashboard.

What are the benefits of joining more directs?

1. You will get 500 from all your directs.
2. More directs helps you in increasing your team.
3. When your directs enter in middle plan or end plan after you, they will give you that entry amount.

Is it compulsory to upgrade to 2 nd level in 1 month?

Yes, if you are on 1st level after 1 month, your sponsor can apply for transfer your Id to another person. In this process, you will lost your Id.

If wrong mobile number filled or lost mobile number, how to change ?

We accept request for change/Changes from registered mobile number or email only. You are requested to update your email and phone number both in your profile. If you lost one, (email/mobile) you can give request from other option.

What to do, if upline has not upgraded my Id after getting payment ?

You have to submit proofs of payment and medium selected. Your Id will be upgraded and upline will be blocked. If your proofs are found wrong, your Id will be blocked.

If upline is not reachable, how to upgrade ?

If receiver (upline) is not reachable, you can pay to us , your Id will be upgraded on behalf of upline and amount will be claimed by upline, when he is in reach. Send payment proof and details of both ids via email on s2feducationaltrust@gmail.com .We will pay via account to upline when he claims from registered number.
Note :- Pay via Paytm only. Send payment proof and details of both ids via email on s2feducationaltrust@gmail.com.We will pay via Paytm to upline when he claim from registered number.

I want to transfer my Id to other. Is it possible?

Yes, you have to apply for transfer from registered mobile number or email only. User can apply for any changes in their profile.

Some of my payment from current level are pending. Can I upgrade for next level?

Your upgrade depends on earning. If your earning is sufficient for upgrade, option will be available to you. Your pending payments will definitely comes to you, when your downline upgrade.

My referral link is not working. My Login I’d is – “ abcd(space)xyz “. What to do?

Web address doesn’t recognise “ spaces” . If your link containing blank space, please replace it with “ %20 “.It will definitely work. If possible, avoid blank space in UserID at time of registration.
Example:- http://server.support2friends/Registration.php?SponsorName

If I stopped working or not upgrade to next level, what will happen?

If You want to receive payment of particular level, you have to upgrade for that level. Otherwise, payment will be skipped to higher upline. It will affect you only. Neither your uplines or your downline affected by this.
