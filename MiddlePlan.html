Intermediate Plan

Registration Process :

No new registration for this plan. Anyone can enter in this plan manually on getting income/earnings more than or equal to Rs. 5000/-. Anyone can enter in this plan anytime. You have to pay Rs. 5000/- to your 1st upline and on payment approval from your upline, you are active in Intermediate plan also.


1st Level Upgrade of Intermediate Plan : You have to join 2 Persons who was your 1st down line. You will get sponsor Funding Income Rs.5,000/- from that 2 persons (Rs.5,000/- x 2 = Rs.10,000/-). Now you have to pay to your 2nd Upline Rs.6,000/- from your 1st income of Rs.4,000/- for the second level upgradation. Now your Funding income is:-
Rs.10,000/- (-) Rs.6,000/- = Rs.4,000/-(Net Profit)


2nd Level Upgrade Of IntermediatePlan : Now you don't have to join anybody (but if you join then you will get that sponsor Funding income of Rs.4,000/-). Now 4 members of your 2nd level will upgrade their ID (means after entering 8 in their down line, they will upgrade their ID), you will get Rs. 6,000/- (Rs.6,000/- x 4 = Rs.24,000/-). Now from that Rs.12,000/- you will have to pay to your 3rd Upline of Rs.12,000/- for the 3rd level upgrade. Now your total Funding incomes is:-
Rs.24,000/- (-) Rs.12,000/- = Rs. 12,000/- (Net Profit).


3rd Level Upgrade Of IntermediatePlan : Now also you don't have to join anybody (but if you join then you will get that sponsor Funding income of Rs.12,000/-). Now 8 members of your 3rd level will upgrade their ID (Just like you upgrade on getting 4 payments of Rs. 6000/- means after entering 16(4th Level) & 32(5th Level) & 64(6th Level) in their downline, they will upgrade their ID), you will get Funding income of Rs.12,000/- (Rs.12,000/- x 8 = Rs.96,000/-). Now from that Rs. 96,000/- you will have to pay to your 4th Upline of Rs. 40,000/- for the 4th level upgrade. Now your total Funding income is:-
Rs.96,000/- (-) Rs.48,000/- = Rs.48,000/- (Net Profit).

Note:-At 3rd level you have to pay Rs. 8000/- to NGO for helping needy people.


4th Level Upgrade Of Intermediate Plan : Now also you don't have to join anybody (but if you join then you will get that sponsor Funding income of Rs.48,000/-). Now 16 members of your 4th level will upgrade their ID (Just like you upgrade on getting 8 payments of Rs. 900/- means after entering 32(5th Level), 64(6th Level) & 128(7th Level) & 256(8th Level below 128) in their down line, they will upgrade their ID), you will get Funding income of Rs. 48,000/- (Rs.40,000/- x 16 = Rs.6,40,000/-). Now from that Rs.6,40,000/- you will have to pay to your 5th Upline of Rs. 30,000/- for the 5th level upgrade. Now your total Funding income is:- br /> Rs.7,68,000/- (-) Rs.3,84000/- = Rs.3,84,000/- (Net Profit).

Note:-Note:-At 4th level you have to pay Rs. 20000/- to NGO for helping needy people.


5th Level Upgrade Of Intermediate Plan : Now also you don't have to join anybody (but if you join then you will get that sponsor Funding income of Rs.3,20,000/-). Now 32 members of your 5th level will upgrade their ID (Just like you upgrade on getting 16 payments of Rs. 40,000/- means after entering 64(6th Level), 128(7th Level), 256(8th Level) & 512(9th Level) & 1024(10th Level Below 512) in their down line, they will upgrade their ID), you will get Funding Income of Rs.3,00,000/- (Rs.3,00,000/- x 32 = Rs.96,00,000/-). Now from that Rs.96,00,000/- you don't have to pay to anybody. Your Earnings end here with total profit nearby Rs. 1 Cr. Now your total Funding income is:-
Rs. 1,22,88000/- (-) Rs.0/- = Rs1,22,88000/- (Net Profit).

Notes To Be Kept In Mind

You have to deposit Rs. 5000/- to your sponsors Bank account for the activation & promotion to other for joining and upload slip.

If you want to take level income then you have to upgrade that particular level. For Example:- you wants helping income of Rs.40,000/- from 16 members(In your 4th level downline) you need to pay first Rs. 40,000/- to your 4th Upline. Only then you will able to get same income. So pay first to 1 and ready to get from many.

Payment to NGO in 3rd and 4th level is compulsory. If member fails to pay these Charges, his/her next payments will be blocked till the payment of these charges.

Member can upgrade to next level whenever his total earning is greater than or equal to the next level upgrade fee.
